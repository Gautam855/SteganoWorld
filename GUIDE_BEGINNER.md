# 🌟 SteganoWorld: Beginner's Guide

Welcome to **SteganoWorld**! This is a powerful, secure platform for private communication and hiding secrets within images.

## 1. What is SteganoWorld?
SteganoWorld is a website where you can:
*   **Chat Privately**: Send messages that only you and the recipient can read. Even the website owners cannot see your messages!
*   **Hide Secrets in Images**: Put text or other images inside a normal-looking photo. This is called *Steganography*.

## 2. Why is it secure?
We use a **Zero-Knowledge** system. This means:
*   **Your Privacy is Yours**: Your messages are locked (encrypted) on your phone or computer *before* they are sent to our server.
*   **No Master Keys**: We don't have a "master key." If someone hacks our server, all they will find is gibberish data that they can't read.
*   **Private Keys**: Your "Secret Key" used for unlocking messages stays on your device and is never uploaded.

## 3. Key Features
*   **Secure E2EE Chat**: Real-time messaging with end-to-end encryption.
*   **Image Encoding**: Hide a message or a photo inside another photo.
*   **Secure Links**: share your secret images via safe, temporary links.
*   **Zero-Knowledge Login**: Login without a traditional password using high-tech security keys.

## 4. How to Use
1.  **Register**: Create an account. Your device will automatically create your security keys.
2.  **Add Friends**: Search for users by their username.
3.  **Send Messages**: Just type and send! The encryption happens automatically.
4.  **Hide Data**: Go to the "Encode" section, upload a photo, type your secret, and download the new "Stego" image.
